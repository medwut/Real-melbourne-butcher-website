Our Story

This begins all the way back in November 1965 When Owners Margaret and Greg opened the Melbourne Butcher Shop on Flinder Street It was one of the first of its kind with brand new cooling equipment.This lead to a boost in customers and product longevity as store owners could make products last for longer and still taste the same way. Customers Loved the taste of homemade seasoned and cut meats. Margaret growing up on a farm was built for this business while her partner Greg was the brains of the operation finding new ways to introduce awesome and tasteful products to the streets of melbourne from all over the world. He imported cheeses from Switzerland and got fine Wagyu Beef from the finest Japanese farms. Now at the fine age of 67 and 69 Margaret and Gregg have chosen to retire but still are very interested in giving melbourne a taste of their original homemade seasoned meat. They have chosen to run a website to further expand their reach and influence the next generation of small family owned businesses through their experiences.
